02 Location & Context

Tablas Island & Alcantara, Romblon

Alcantara is a municipality on Tablas Island, the largest island in the Province of Romblon, Philippines. Romblon is situated in the MIMAROPA region and is known for natural landscapes, clear coastal waters and a relatively untouched environment.

Tourism GrowthRomblon is an emerging destination with growing tourism and eco-development interest.
AccessibilityTablas Island is accessible by both air through San Agustin Airport and by sea.
Government PolicyNational policy continues to support island-based tourism and sustainable development.
ScarcityLimited large-scale titled landholdings of this scale are available in the region.
SeaRegular ferry service to/from Batangas Port, approximately 4–5 hours.
AirLight aircraft service to San Agustin Airport, Tablas Island.
RoadRoad networks within Alcantara connecting barangays.
